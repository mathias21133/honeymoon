Pre-Departure Checklist

🔴 Critical Bookings — Do Immediately

  • Khao Sok 2D/1N overnight (May 27–28) Book 4–6 weeks before departure · elephanthills.com or ourjunglehouse.com · Confirm Cheow Lan Lake package + 8am pickup from The Shore
  • Hakkoku omakase (Tokyo, June 8 dinner) Opens exactly 2 months before on omakase.in · Set reminder for April 8, 2026 · Miss the window = it's gone
  • Koffee Mameya Kakeru (Tokyo, June 7 morning) tablecheck.com · Book ASAP, very limited daily slots
  • Hutong (Hong Kong, June 1 dinner) hutong.com.hk or OpenTable · 4–6 weeks out · Mention honeymoon · The Peking duck anchor dinner
  • Ho Lee Fook (Hong Kong, May 29 dinner) holeefook.com.hk · 1–2 weeks out · Modern Cantonese, Causeway Bay
  • Aqua Luna Junk Cruise (Hong Kong, May 29 evening) Klook · 1–2 weeks out · 7:30 or 7:45pm sailing · Sells out on weekends
  • Torisho Sai (Kyoto, June 3 dinner) Michelin yakitori · Book ASAP via Tableall or Pocket Concierge

🟡 Book 1–3 Weeks Before Departure

  • Yakiniku Jumbo Hanare (Tokyo, June 10 dinner) Tableall.com or Pocket Concierge · 2–3 weeks out · Mention honeymoon
  • S'accapau (Tokyo, June 11 dinner) Ask Prince Sakura Tower concierge to call · Small Italian basement · Backup: Pizza Strada walk-in
  • Cokuun (Tokyo, June 10 morning coffee) Book 1–2 weeks ahead via website/Instagram · Confirm current location first
  • Izakaya Cooking Class (Tokyo, June 7 evening) Airbnb Experiences / Viator · 2–3 weeks out
  • Gion Duck Noodles (Kyoto, June 4 dinner) AutoReserve or arrive at 5pm · 9 seats, Tabelog Top West Japan
  • Hikoniku Tocome (Kyoto, June 5 dinner) Book 4–6 weeks out · Charcoal beef patties, Gion machiya
  • Peak Tram (Hong Kong, May 30 morning) thepeak.com.hk · Buy online to skip queue · Round-trip + Sky Terrace combo
  • Macau Ferry (Hong Kong, June 1) turbojet.com.hk · Round-trip, 9am departure
  • Suay Restaurant (Phuket, May 28 if doing Old Town dinner) suayrestaurant.com · 1 week out · Best sit-down dinner in Old Town

📋 Documents & Digital Setup — Complete Before Departure

Required Pre-Arrival Registration

  • TDAC — Thailand Digital Arrival Card thaiPass.go.th · One per traveler · Complete before flying from NY · Required on arrival
  • Visit Japan Web registration vjw-lp.digital.go.jp · 2 weeks before arrival · Both travelers register individually · QR codes skip paper forms at immigration

eSIM & Connectivity

  • Thailand eSIM Airalo (~$5–8 for Thailand) or True Move H SIM at HKT airport (~฿300–400)
  • Hong Kong eSIM Airalo or Nomad (~$5–8 for HK data) or 7-Eleven SIM at airport (~HK$50)
  • Japan eSIM Airalo (~$22/10GB) or MobiMatter (~$14/20GB NTT Docomo) · Install, don't activate until landing

Payments & Transit

  • Suica (Japan transit) Add to Apple Wallet before departure · Load ¥3,000–5,000 on arrival · Works on all lines
  • Notify credit cards Alert cards for Thailand, Hong Kong, Japan travel May 24–June 12
  • ATM plan Thailand: ฿6,000–8,000 cash (Kasikorn/Bangkok Bank ATMs) · HK: HK$3,000–5,000 (HSBC/Hang Seng) · Japan: ¥50,000–80,000 (7-Eleven ATMs)
  • Octopus Card (Hong Kong) Pick up at MTR customer service on arrival · Load HK$200–300

Passport & Travel Insurance

  • Passport validity check Must be valid 6+ months beyond June 12, 2026
  • Passport copies Photo ID pages + email to yourself · Needed for tax-free shopping in Japan
  • Travel insurance Confirm coverage for Thailand, Hong Kong, Japan · Medical + trip interruption

🎒 Packing Essentials

Documents (Carry-On Only)

  • Passports (both)
  • Printed hotel confirmations (The Shore, Island Shangri-La, Suiran, Prince Sakura Tower)
  • Printed flight confirmations (all segments)
  • Credit cards (2+ backups)
  • Emergency contact list

Tech & Power

  • Universal travel adapter (Type A/B/C for Thailand, Type G for HK, Type A/B for Japan)
  • Phone + charger
  • Camera + charger (if bringing)
  • Power bank (essential for Suica/Apple Pay in Japan)
  • Headphones
  • E-reader / iPad

Clothing — Tropical/Summer

  • Light layers (27–34°C across all destinations)
  • Comfortable walking shoes (Fushimi Inari hike, Tsukiji, city walking)
  • Sandals / slides for beach (Phuket)
  • Swimwear (Phuket beach + Khao Sok lake)
  • Sun hat / cap
  • Sunglasses
  • Light rain jacket or packable umbrella (Tsuyu season in Japan, afternoon showers everywhere)
  • 1–2 nicer outfits for upscale dinners (Hutong, Hakkoku, S'accapau)

Toiletries & Health

  • Sunscreen (SPF 50+)
  • Insect repellent (Khao Sok jungle)
  • Motion sickness meds (Aqua Luna cruise, Macau ferry)
  • Personal meds (bring 1.5x duration in carry-on)
  • Basic first aid (band-aids, pain relievers)
  • Hand sanitizer
  • Lip balm

Nice-to-Haves

  • Small daypack / tote for daily excursions
  • Waterproof phone case (Khao Sok, beach)
  • Notebook + pen (travel journal)
  • Ziploc bags (wet clothes, snacks)
  • Laundry detergent packets (5-night stretches)
  • Earplugs + eye mask (flights, time zone adjustment)

✈️ Flight & Transfer Confirmations

  • May 24 departure Confirm flight time from US → Phuket (HKT)
  • May 29: Phuket → Hong Kong Confirm airport transfer from The Shore night before · HKT ~45 min from Kata Noi · Allow 2.5 hrs at airport
  • June 3: Hong Kong → Kyoto 10:55am flight HKG → confirm arrival transport to Suiran · Complimentary taxi from Kyoto Station — book 3+ days ahead at +81-75-872-0101
  • June 6: Kyoto → Tokyo (Shinkansen) ~2h15m · Book reserved seats via Smart EX app or JR Ticket Office
  • June 12 departure: Tokyo → US 6pm flight from Haneda (Terminal 3) · Leave Prince Sakura Tower by 3pm · Keikyu Line from Shibuya ~45 min (~¥600)

📞 On-Arrival Tasks

Phuket (May 24)

  • Withdraw ฿6,000–8,000 cash at airport ATM
  • Activate Thailand eSIM
  • Book Spa Session 1 (May 25, 3pm) and Session 2 (May 28, 2:30pm) at Serenity Spa desk
  • Reserve Param Para dinner (May 25 or May 27) at front desk
  • Confirm Khao Sok pickup time 48 hrs before (May 25) — 8am May 27

Hong Kong (May 29)

  • Withdraw HK$3,000–5,000 at HSBC/Hang Seng ATM
  • Pick up Octopus Card at MTR Admiralty station, load HK$200–300
  • Activate Hong Kong eSIM

Kyoto (June 3)

  • Activate Japan eSIM
  • Confirm reserved Shinkansen seats to Tokyo (June 6)

Tokyo (June 6)

  • Withdraw ¥50,000–80,000 at 7-Eleven ATM
  • Load Suica ¥3,000–5,000 via Apple Wallet
  • Confirm all Tokyo restaurant reservations with concierge
  • Confirm June 12 departure transport night before
