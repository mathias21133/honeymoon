Hotel — Two Options

The Prince Sakura Tower Tokyo, Autograph Collection · Takanawa

Location + Transit

Takanawa, just south of Shinagawa Station. Shibuya is 6 min on the Yamanote Line. Shinjuku is ~20 min. Haneda is 45 min on the Keikyu Line directly from Shinagawa — the best possible base for a stress-free Day 7 departure. The hotel sits in the quieter Takanawa garden district rather than in the middle of the action, which for a honeymoon base is a feature, not a bug.

Coffee Note

The Takanawa area has no specialty coffee from your list within walking distance — the nearest serious option is Onibus Coffee in Nakameguro, one stop away (~7 min). On early mornings (Day 4 Tsukiji), Blue Bottle Coffee inside Shinagawa Station is the practical first coffee. On all other days, coffee comes after the first train — always first stop before any activity.

Open Questions + Not Yet Locked In

Things That Still Need a Decision

  • Teppanyaki (Azabudai Nakamura vs Ginza Ukai-Tei) — Azabudai Nakamura is the recommendation (live seafood, chef counter performance, Kobe wagyu). Day 6 is the natural home. Book via tableall.com 3–4 weeks out.
  • Sushi (Hakkoku vs Harutaka) — Hakkoku is the pick for your style (à la carte, Jiro-caliber, English spoken, omakase.in). Harutaka if you want the full omakase experience at ¥60–70k/person — phone-only, ask hotel concierge to call.
  • Museum of Narratives — Flagged from your spreadsheet. Confirm location — potentially fits Day 6 alongside Mori Art Museum if Roppongi-adjacent.
  • Baseball Game — Flagged from your spreadsheet. Tokyo Giants likely have home games at Tokyo Dome in June. Check npb.jp — could slot into Day 4 or Day 6 as an afternoon option. Easy Yamanote access.
  • Sumo Wrestling — Nagoya Basho is mid-July, not June. No tournament in Tokyo during your dates. Arashio Stable in Nihonbashi sometimes allows morning practice viewing — worth asking hotel concierge if interested.
  • Tsukiji vs Toyosu — Not competing. Tsukiji Outer Market is a morning street food crawl (your style — Day 4). Toyosu is where the wholesale auction moved in 2018 — only worth the 5am pilgrimage for the tuna auction (book months ahead). Skip Toyosu unless you want to do the auction specifically.
  • Halal Wagyu Stand Shibuya — 北田ビル, 29-3 Udagawacho, Shibuya — right in the heart of Shibuya, easy late-night slot on Day 1 or any Shibuya-base evening.

📋 Practical Notes

MoneyWithdraw ¥50,000–80,000 cash at 7-Eleven ATM on arrival. Cards work almost everywhere except small ramen shops, some izakayas, Tsukiji stalls.
Transit (Suica)Add Suica to Apple Wallet. Works on all lines including Yamanote. Load ¥3,000–5,000. Keep a power bank — dead phone = no transit.
eSIMBuy before departure — Airalo (~$22/10GB) or MobiMatter (~$14/20GB on NTT Docomo). Install, don't activate until landing.
Entry / Visit Japan WebRegister 2 weeks before arrival at vjw-lp.digital.go.jp. Free, official. QR codes at immigration — skips paper forms. Both travelers register individually.
Tax-Free Shopping10% back on purchases over ¥5,000 per store. Must carry actual passport — copies don't work. Worth doing on knife / clothing days.
